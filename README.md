🎨 Task 3: Data Visualization — Where Numbers Tell Stories
"Data is the new oil, but visualization is the engine that makes it run."

🚀 What I Built
I transformed raw rating data into 2 interactive visual stories that reveal customer sentiment patterns:

Visualization	What It Shows	Why It Matters
📊 Bar Chart	Rating distribution (1-5 stars)	Spot the most common ratings instantly
🥧 Pie Chart	Percentage breakdown	See the big picture in one glance
🎯 Key Insights Discovered
⭐ 5-star ratings dominate: 4 out of 15 products got perfect scores

🔥 Most products are beloved: 60% of reviews are 4-5 stars

⚠️ One outlier: Cable got the only 1-star (needs investigation!)

python
📦 pandas → Data manipulation
🎨 matplotlib → Beautiful visualizations
🖼️ PNG (300 DPI) → Print-ready quality
📁 Files Generated

rating_pie.png — Pie chart showing percentage distribution
